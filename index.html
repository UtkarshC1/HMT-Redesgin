The Chronology

1953

The Agreement

The Government of India signs a technical collaboration with Citizen Watch Co., Japan. The dream of an Indian watchmaker is born.

1961

The Janata Era

Prime Minister Nehru releases the first batch of Hand Wound watches. The Janata becomes the unofficial uniform of the Indian civil servant.

1975

Going Automatic

HMT introduces the Rajat and Kanchan automatic series (Day-Date), bringing luxury mechanics to the common man.

1985

Quartz Revolution

Adapting to the times, HMT launches quartz watches. The Sona continues to dominate as the premier wedding gift.

2016

The Preservation

The factories wind down, but the legend lives on. The Heritage Wing opens to preserve the history for future generations.
